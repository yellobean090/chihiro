“Chihiro — a name that in Japanese signifies a thousand questions. A place that sparks curiosity, wonder, and quiet conversation.”

Tucked inside the lush greens of Lucknow Cantonment, Chihiro was imagined as more than just a café. It was built as a calm, welcoming space where good food, thoughtful design, and warm conversations come together.

Every detail — from the ingredients we use to the way the space feels — has been chosen with care. We bake fresh daily, pull our espresso with intention, and hope you leave feeling a little lighter than when you came in.

Female-led, quietly romantic, and surrounded by old trees and soft afternoon light, Chihiro feels like stepping into a world that was curated with tenderness.
